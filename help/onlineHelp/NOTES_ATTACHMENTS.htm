NOTES AND ATTACHMENTS

The Notes and Attachments section of the School Census form (Figure: Notes and Attachments Section) is used to provide additional notes and comments relevant to the school census record. Additional documents and images may also be uploaded and attached in this section.

 

Figure: Notes and Attachments Section

 

For more details on how to provide the information requested in the Notes and Attachments section, refer to the table below.

 

Field Name

Description

Notes

Provide any additional notes and comments that are relevant to the school census form. For more details, see Managing Notes.

Attachments

Attach documents and images that may contain relevant information. For more details, see Managing Attachments.
