NON-TEACHING STAFF INFORMATION

The Non-teaching Staff Information section of the School Census form (Figure: Non-teaching Staff Information Section) is available for basic schools only and is used to provide information about all non-teaching staff enrolled in school.

 

Figure: Non-teaching Staff Information Section

 

For more details on how to provide the information requested in the Non-teaching Staff Information section, refer to the table below.

 

Note: Some fields in this section are mandatory to be filled in. They are marked with an asterisk.

 

Field Name

Description

How MANY non-teaching staff do you have in your school?

Indicate the number of male and female non-teaching staff members who are enrolled in school in different capacities. This field is mandatory.
