HISTORY (AUDIT TRAIL)

The History (Audit Trail) section of the School Census form (Figure: History (Audit Trail) Section) stores historical data on changes made to the form and is intended for keeping track of the modifications introduced to the form and associated school census records. Each saved version provides detailed information about the corresponding record, including its creation and management history, information on the date/time when modifications were introduced to it, the modifier details, etc.

 

Figure: History (Audit Trail) Section

 

For more details on what information can be viewed in the History (Audit Trail) section, see the table below.

 

Field Name

Description

Audit Trail

Provides information about the recent changes made to the application form. Identifies the user who performed the changes and the date/time when the modifications were made.
